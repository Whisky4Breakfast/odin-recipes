Dada's Leftover Pizza

Description: Let's face it, you're going to have leftover pizza to eat, often. So let's make that not such a bad thing.

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 slices leftover pizza
  • 2 Tbsp Ranch Dressing
  • 1 Tbsp Hot Sauce

Steps:

  1. Heat pizza in microwave on Med for 3 min, or until hot
  2. Mix ranch and hot sauce in small dip cup
  3. Once pizza is ready, dip and enjoy!
