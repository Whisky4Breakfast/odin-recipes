Dada's Scrambled Eggs!

Description: A lovely preparation of creamy scrambled eggs. Tasty enough for the most critical of toddlers!

Ingredients:

  • 2 Large eggs
  • 2 tsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • Garlic Powder
  • Dried Parsley
  • Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Milk

Steps:

  1. Add oil and butter to pan. Mix and heat on Med until butter begins to simmer
  2. Add garlic and stir until roasted. Remove from heat to cool
  3. Once pan cools, add eggs and mix until whites are dispersed
  4. Cook on low heat, stiring constantly to avoid sticking to pan
  5. Once eggs begin to cook into lumps, add milk and stir together
  6. Once eggs begin to lump once more, add salt and parsley and mix
  7. Cook to desired consistency and Serve!
