Dada's Mac n Cheese

Description: Good old fashioned Mac n Cheese, with some added flavor from a few key seasonings. So good, even twins will eat it!

Ingredients:

  • Dry Noodles
  • Cheese Sause
  • Garlic Powder
  • 6 cups Water
  • 1 Tbs Butter
  • Dried Parsley

Steps:

  1. Boil Water
  2. Add Noodles and Boil for 8-10min
  3. Strain Noodles
  4. While noodles strain, Melt butter in pan and roast garlic
  5. Once garlic roasted, mix in cheese sauce and stir
  6. Once mixed, return noodles to pan and mix together
  7. Lastly, add dried parsley, mix, and Serve!
